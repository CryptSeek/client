What is CryptSeek?

CryptSeek is metadata-less messaging app!

Messages aren't stored on our server, meaning everything stays between you and the people you message.

This also applies to the friends system, unique tokens allow connections between users with no data stored.

Messages are encrypted to add a layer of security.
