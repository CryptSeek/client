What is CryptSeek?

CryptSeek is an end-to-end encrypted messaging app!

Better yet, messages aren't stored on our server.

In fact, nothing about your message activity can be tracked, meaning everything stays between you and the people you message.

This also applies to the friends system, you are in full control of your CryptSeek identity

Every part of your messages, including the sender and receiver, is end-to-end encrypted
